## Full description draft

`Oh Craps! brings strategy-focused craps reference tools to Android with accessibility-first design.

Browse a large strategy library and quickly find what you need with search, table minimum filters, and buy-in filters. Open any strategy to review clearly structured content with headings, notes, credit, and step-by-step instructions.

Create your own strategies in the app, then save, edit, duplicate, delete, and resubmit them whenever you want. Share both built-in and user-created strategies using the native Android share sheet with plain text output.

The app also includes a Rules section with expandable topics, plus an About section with references and feedback options.

Oh Craps! is designed for clear navigation, predictable focus behavior, and strong support for Android accessibility features, including TalkBack workflows across core tasks.`
